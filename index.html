FAQ

What platforms are supported?

Windows (x64) and macOS (Apple Silicon/Intel). Linux build available on request.

Where is my data stored?

Locally on your device (e.g., Windows: %LOCALAPPDATA%\FastApply). Sensitive fields are encrypted.

Do I need an account?

Yes. Sign in inside the app to enable usage limits and license checks.
